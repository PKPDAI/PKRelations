PK REx Labelling Instructions

Quick Reminders!

  1. Start by annotating entities and, once finished, relations
  2. Press the green arrow to move onto the next example
  3. Remember to save your annotations every now and then by pressing Ctrl+S or the save button
  4. You can go back to the last 10 examples you labelled and change your answers
  5. To go back click on the example in the history tab or press the back arrow
  6. You cannot go back more than 10 tables as results are saved!
